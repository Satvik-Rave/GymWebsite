About Us

DVS FIT provides a 24/7 Fitness facility to residents of Punjab, as well as surrounding areas to help people reach and maintain their goals. We combine different types of fitness equipment to meet different fitness needs and levels. At DVS FIT you will find all the latest strength and cardio equipment along with a energetic group exercise program that includes Pound, Zumba, Kickboxing, Bootcamp, Muscle Building and many other cardio classes. You will find a supportive environment with all kinds of people who are working just as hard as you to meet their goals. Our Staff, Trainers and Group exercise instructors are committed to offering our members a great fitness experience. Whether you are a mom looking to get back into shape, a marathon runner trying to shave a few minutes off your personal best or just trying to stay healthy we would love to help you realize your potential and meet your goals!!
